Castle Code

Rules every hunter must follow

01

Respect All Members

Treat every castle member with respect regardless of their rank or background. Harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior will result in immediate removal.

02

No Spoilers Without Tags

Use spoiler tags when discussing recent chapters or episodes. Respect members who haven't caught up to the latest content.

03

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to Solo Leveling or the designated channel topics. Off-topic conversations should be moved to appropriate channels.

04

No Spam or Self-Promotion

Avoid excessive posting, caps lock, or promotional content without permission. Quality over quantity in all interactions.

05

Follow Discord ToS

All Discord Terms of Service apply. Members must be 13+ years old and follow all platform guidelines.
