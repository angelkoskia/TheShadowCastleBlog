Castle Code

Rules every hunter must follow

1

Respect All Members

Treat all members with respect and kindness. Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

2

No Spamming or Advertising

Spamming messages, links, or advertisements is not allowed. This includes excessive self-promotion.

3

Stay on Topic

Keep discussions relevant to Solo Leveling and the interests of the community. Use direct messages for personal conversations.

4

Follow Discord's Terms of Service

Adhere to all of Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. Familiarize yourself with them if you haven't already.
